Parking Lot System

About

A parking lot system is implemented using a DBMS to manage vehicle number plates, check-in/out times, and billing. Use a database schema with tables for vehicles, parking sessions, and bills. Track vehicles with their number plates, record check-in times upon entry, and update with check-out times upon exit. Calculate parking duration to determine costs based on an hourly rate stored in the system. Generate bills showing the total amount due for each parking session. Ensure efficient querying for real-time status updates and historical reporting. This system ensures accurate tracking and billing for parking activities using structured database management principles and also has a monthly pass system.
